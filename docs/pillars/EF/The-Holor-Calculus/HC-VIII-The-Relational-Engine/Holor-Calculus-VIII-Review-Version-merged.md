## 2. Model 0.5 - 1D HSE with a "Torsion Bump"

### 2.1 Setup

- Awareness manifold: $M=[0,1]$ with coordinate $x$.

- HSE reduces to:

$$
\frac{d}{d x} \Phi(x)+T_{\chi}(x)-R_{e}(x)=0 .

Pick:

- Structural torsion: a constant $T_{\chi, \text { struct }}=T_{0}$ (e.g. a corpus with mild baseline twist everywhere).
- Use-induced torsion: a bump around some focal node $x_{0} \in(0,1)$ where the user has been circling:

$$
\frac{d}{d x} \Phi(x)=R_{0}-T_{0}-A \exp \left(-\frac{\left(x-x_{0}\right)^{2}}{2 \sigma^{2}}\right) .

### 2.2 Solve HSE

Integrate from 0 to $x$ :

$$
\Phi(x)=\Phi(0)+\left(R_{0}-T_{0}\right) x-A \int_{0}^{x} \exp \left(-\frac{\left(s-x_{0}\right)^{2}}{2 \sigma^{2}}\right) d s

The integral is an error-function bump:

$$
\int_{0}^{x} \exp \left(-\frac{\left(s-x_{0}\right)^{2}}{2 \sigma^{2}}\right) d s=\sigma \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}\left[\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{x-x_{0}}{\sqrt{2} \sigma}\right)-\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{-x_{0}}{\sqrt{2} \sigma}\right)\right] .

$$
\Phi(x)=\Phi(0)+\left(R_{0}-T_{0}\right) x-A \sigma \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}\left[\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{x-x_{0}}{\sqrt{2} \sigma}\right)-\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{-x_{0}}{\sqrt{2} \sigma}\right)\right] .
$$

Interpretation:

- Away from $x_{0}, \Phi(x)$ is approximately linear in $x$ with slope $R_{0}-T_{0}$.
- Around $x_{0}$, the attention-induced torsion bump produces a local kink in the awareness flow: $\Phi$ bends in response to the user's repeated spiraling around that node.
