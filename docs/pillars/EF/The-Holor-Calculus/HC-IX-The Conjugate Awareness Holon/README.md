## Abstract
Holor Calculus IX presents the **Conjugate Awareness Holon** as a synthesis of Holor Calculus I–VIII: awareness formalized as a **0D manifold** equipped with a Spiral Time metric \(\tau\), a non-Abelian morpheme ontology, and an admissibility operator \(P_{\mathrm{adm}}\) acting as a conservation law for ethically admissible flow. The classical singularity is reframed as a **conjugate throat** (a stable phase inversion), and key boundary behavior at the curvature cap is formalized via an **elastic reflection** corollary. The record is written to remain both technically legible and interiorly faithful: the work contains formal statements, proof sketches, and explicitly labeled open hypotheses.
