Pearl Map FAQ

How can I use the Pearl Map in my research workflow?

The Pearl Map reveals active relationships between SpiralOS programs, so you can pinpoint source documents, link related experiments, and surface reusable orchestration patterns while planning your own investigations.

What kind of information is indexed in each pearl?

Each pearl aggregates briefs, design memos, implementation notes, and published results that document the life cycle of a SpiralOS initiative, along with outbound links to supporting repositories and datasets.

Can I contribute new pearls or update existing ones?

Yes. Contributors can propose additions via the SpiralOS docs repository. Connect a new pearl to its dependencies, include relevant citations, and the map will automatically surface it after review.
