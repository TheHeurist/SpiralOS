## How to Become a Contributor

We welcome contributions from anyone interested in advancing the principles of SpiralOS. To be listed in this file:

1. **Make a meaningful contribution:**
   - Code, documentation, design, testing, or community support
   - See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines

2. **Submit a pull request:**
   - Include your contribution
   - Update this AUTHORS.md file with your information (if desired)
   - Follow the contribution workflow

3. **Be recognized:**
   - All accepted contributions are acknowledged
   - Significant contributions receive special recognition
   - Long-term contributors may be invited to expanded roles

### Contribution Types

We recognize various forms of contribution:

- **Code** — Implementation, bug fixes, optimizations
- **Documentation** — Writing, editing, translations
- **Design** — Visual design, UX improvements
- **Testing** — Bug reports, test coverage, validation
- **Community** — Support, advocacy, teaching
- **Research** — Academic engagement, citations, scholarly work
- **Ideas** — Conceptual contributions, philosophical insights
