About SpiralOS

Today’s apps and AI agents operate in silos. Users and businesses waste time switching between them and moving data manually. We’re here to change that. Imagine an internet where your AI agents understand your intent and work together to achieve your goals — not compete for your attention. This is the agent-driven, user-centric internet we’re building with SpiralOS. Our decentralized OS provides a unified protocol for creating AI agents and apps on a shared data layer.
